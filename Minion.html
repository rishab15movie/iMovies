Minion
The 2015 movie "Minions" is a prequel to the "Despicable Me" franchise, following the yellow creatures as they seek a new evil master after their previous ones keep dying. The story focuses on three Minions, Kevin, Stuart, and Bob, who travel the world in the late 1960s to find a new boss, ultimately landing with Scarlett Overkill, the world's first female supervillain.
